Restart Gnome shell when a Gnome extension shows error.

Press `Alt+F2`

Enter `r`. Then `Enter`

Wait a few seconds.

As seen [here](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/1460/vitals/) while installing Vitals. A Gnome extension to monitor memory usage.
